HESUPESU NETWORK SAFETY GUIDE

Wikipedia defines information security as "Information security is the maintenance of the availability, confidentiality and integrity of information. The information to be secured can take several forms. These include digital records, physical records and the knowledge of people, such as employees. Information security also applies to the protection of information during its transmission." In 2021, every employee will be affected by information security and should have the basics down pat.

On this site we will go through the most important employees of Hesupesu Oy employee's data security requirements. This package includes information on how to and general information on mobile and online security. Each Hesupesu employee is expected to read this manual.
