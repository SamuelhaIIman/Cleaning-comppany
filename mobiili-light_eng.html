MOBILE SECURITY

What to do if you lose your phone?

If the lost device is a work phone, contact your employer immediately. Hesupesu Oy will replace the lost or or broken work phone. With your own device, do the following:

  1. Try calling your phone. It is always possible that you have left your phone somewhere and someone has it. found it.
  2. Try to locate your phone. Apple phones can be tracked on your computer by logging in with your Apple ID www.icloud.com-palveluun and from there select Find my iPhone. Windows phones can be located in the same way by logging in with your Microsoft ID to www.microsoft.com and selecting View Account from My Info and then selecting Phone. For Android phones, Samsung has its own location service at findmymobile.samsung.com. For others, For other brands, a separate application, such as Android Device Manager, is required.
  3. If your phone has been stolen or you suspect it has been misused, report it to your operator and hang up. your subscription temporarily. The emergency numbers for the major operators are:
    Elisa +358 10 190 240
    DNA +358 44 144 044
    Telia +358 200 17000

    4. You can also temporarily close your subscription on your operator's online service using your login details. For Elisa the online service is Oma Elisa.

  4. A stolen phone must be reported to the police. You can do this online at: poliisi.fi/rikokset. To report the theft, you will need the phone's imei code, which can be found in the phone's packaging. Remember to ask for a copy of the crime report for the insurance company. It is advisable to block your phone's connection/ SIM card permanently and get a new one.
How can I prevent problems caused by disappearances?
  1. In addition to the pin code prompt, set a security code or fingerprint sensor on your phone. This prevents a thief from getting in access your subscription.
  2. Make sure the location service on your phone is turned on. If your phone does not support as a standard feature, download the appropriate app.
  3. To recover your phone's imei code, dial *#06# on your phone in call mode. Travel abroad you should also write down the operator's emergency number where you can close your connection.
  4. On your operator's website, create an account to sign in to the service and temporarily close your subscription.
